About ILMATRIX

ILMATRIX is an AI-powered study companion for university students. We help you understand materials, practice with quizzes, draft forum replies, and prepare for exams responsibly.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Make advanced learning assistance accessible, ethical, and effective for every student.

Our Vision

A future where students learn faster and deeper with AI that respects integrity and academic standards.

Our Story

ILMATRIX was created to solve a simple problem: studying from diverse materials can be overwhelming. By bringing together document parsing (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and images via OCR) and fast, reliable AI models, we deliver clear, contextual help without replacing your own learning process.

Our Values

  • Integrity

    We promote honest learning and discourage misuse.

  • Clarity

    We explain with structure, citing from your materials where possible.

  • Accessibility

    Fast to start, easy to use, and works well on serverless.

  • Performance

    Powered by Groq for low-latency responses.

  • Privacy

    Uploads are processed for study use; bring your own API key backend.

  • Support

    We iterate with your feedback to improve the experience.

Technology

  • Hono.js backend with serverless-ready adapters.
  • Groq SDK using Meta Llama models.
  • Robust extractors: pdfjs-dist, tesseract.js OCR, and DOCX/PPTX via JSZip.
